About

Hi, I'm Patti! I'm a developer living in sunny Las Vegas, Nevada. I’m a bit of a nerd of sorts. I have a Bachelor’s of Science degree in professional communication. That degree has helped my career in so many different aspects. However, my real passion is web development. Combining my experience with communication and web development has created the ultimate tech superpower (in my mind anyways). I can communicate efficiently with clientele while delivering clean and clear-cut code. I’m both driven and self-motivated. I’m constantly experimenting with new technologies and techniques.

What draws me to web development? I’m glad you asked! I love collaboration, plain and simple. The idea of working with a team of developers to connect all the moving parts is exciting to me. So, with that being said, let’s create something epic together!

When not plugging away at something awesome on the computer, you will likely find me: hanging out with the fam, loving on my two dogs, beating my hubs at Halo, or lost in my next Netflix binge.

Portfolio

Singapore Quiz App

This was one of my very first projects that I created. It was built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and JQuery. It’s a single page application that tests the user’s knowledge (or lack thereof) of random Singapore facts.

The opening screen prompts the user to start the quiz. As the user moves through each question they have instant feedback and score tally generated. While navigating through the quiz the user is met with vibrant and engaging colors and graphics.
